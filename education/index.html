Most of my teaching and supervision at Uppsala University fails within one of my research areas or in the field of software engineering and project management.

Uppsala and prospective students:
If you are keen on pursuing a research and/or development project for a master/bachelor thesis or a course project feel free to get in touch! You can find the updated list of project proposals for the next period below, or at the website of the UU-InfoLab research group. Normally, students must have spent at least a semester at Uppsala University and are required to have taken at least one course in either data science (such as 1DL007, 1DL370, 1MS031, 1RT003) or in software engineering (such as 1DL251, 1DL242) relevant for the project.

Summer interships:
I am no longer hosting summer interns.
